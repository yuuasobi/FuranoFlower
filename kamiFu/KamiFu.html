About Kami-Furano

🗺️

Perfect Location

Located in central Hokkaido between Biei and Furano, Kami-Furano offers the perfect balance of accessibility and tranquility. Just 2.5 hours from Sapporo and 45 minutes from Asahikawa Airport.

🌄

Mountain Views

Situated at the foot of the Tokachi Mountain Range, the town offers breathtaking views of Mount Tokachi and surrounding peaks, creating a dramatic backdrop for your adventures.

🌾

Agricultural Paradise

Known for its rich agricultural heritage, Kami-Furano produces some of Hokkaido's finest crops including potatoes, wheat, and the famous Furano melons.

Natural Beauty

💜

Lavender Fields

Home to Japan's largest lavender fields, the town transforms into a purple paradise during summer. The Lavender East fields are a must-see spectacle.

🌅

Stunning Sunrises

Hinode Park offers spectacular sunrise views over the lavender fields, creating magical moments that photographers dream of.

⭐

Starry Nights

With minimal light pollution, Kami-Furano offers incredible stargazing opportunities, especially from the elevated areas around Mount Tokachi.

🏔️

Miyama Pass

Experience 360-degree panoramic views from Miyama Pass, where you can see the entire Furano Basin and surrounding mountains.

Local Cuisine & Specialties

🥩

Kami-Furano Pork

Premium locally-raised pork known for its tender texture and rich flavor. Try it in shabu-shabu or ginger pork dishes.

🍦

Lavender Soft Cream

A unique and refreshing treat made with local lavender, perfect for cooling down on hot summer days.

🌽

Sweet Corn

Exceptionally sweet corn grown in the fertile soil of Kami-Furano, a summer delicacy not to be missed.

🍈

Furano Melons

Juicy and sweet melons that are among the finest in Japan, available during the summer harvest season.

🥔

Premium Potatoes

High-quality potatoes used in local dishes and even distilled into unique potato shochu.

🍷

Local Wine

Visit local wineries to taste wines made from grapes grown in the cool Hokkaido climate.

Things to Do

🚴

Cycling Adventures

Explore the scenic countryside on well-maintained cycling routes, perfect for enjoying the fresh air and beautiful landscapes.

♨️

Hot Springs

Relax in the Tokachi Onsen area, home to some of Hokkaido's highest altitude hot springs with stunning mountain views.

🏕️

Camping

Experience nature up close at Hinode Park Campground, surrounded by lavender fields and mountain vistas.

🎨

Art & Culture

Visit local galleries and the Miyama Pass Art Park to discover the creative spirit of the region.

Getting There

🚄

By Train

Take the JR Furano Line to Kami-Furano Station. Regular services connect from Sapporo and Asahikawa.

🚗

By Car

Route 237 runs through the town, making it easily accessible by car. Scenic drives from Sapporo or Asahikawa.

✈️

By Air

Fly to Asahikawa Airport, then take a 45-minute drive or bus ride to Kami-Furano.
