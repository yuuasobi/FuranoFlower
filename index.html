About FuranoFlower

FuranoFlower is a website that introduces the charm of the Furano area in Hokkaido. We provide detailed information about the characteristics, tourist spots, and gourmet information of each region: Kami-Furano, Naka-Furano, Furano, and Minami-Furano.

Beautiful nature throughout the four seasons, rich food culture, and encounters with warm people. Please create wonderful memories in the Furano area.
